We design a **Anti-robust AI Generated Content Forensics System Based on Noise Characterization**. The system can detect the AI-generated content with high accuracy and low false positive rate, which supports multiple data type inputs, including images, videos, and audios. The system is based on the noise characteristics of AI-generated content, and the noise is extracted by the noise extraction algorithm.
