We propose a novel dataset screening viewpoint for the problem of high cost of training for autonomous driving, where data frames are evaluated from four perspectives: environment, traffic participants, uncertainty, and algorithmic features, and the data that are most likely to provide high added value are screened to form a dataset. Training optimization for image detection algorithms was carried out, and some results were achieved.
